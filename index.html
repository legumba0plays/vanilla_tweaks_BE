Resource Packs Datapacks Crafting Tweaks
Starting from Minecraft Bedrock version 1.16.1 Resource packs can alter textures, sounds and models. For example change your crosshair or select a new menu background. Starting from Minecraft Bedrock version 1.16.1 They can run commands in-game, control loot tables and change crafting recipes. Starting from Minecraft Bedrock version 1.16.1 Crafting Tweaks are a separate category of datapacks. They can change or add crafting recipes and add functionality.
